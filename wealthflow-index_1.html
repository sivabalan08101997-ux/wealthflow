Connect your database

Wealthflow uses Supabase to sync your data across all devices — phone, tablet, laptop — in real time. It's free and takes 2 minutes.

1
Go to supabase.com → Sign up free → Create a new project → Wait ~1 min for it to spin up.
2
In your project: click Settings → API. Copy your Project URL and anon public key.
3
Go to SQL Editor → paste and run the SQL from the SETUP.md file included in this download. (Creates 3 tables.)
4
Paste your URL and key below, click Connect.
Could not connect. Double-check your URL and key.

Your keys are stored only in your browser's localStorage. Data goes directly from your browser to your own Supabase project — Wealthflow never sees it.
